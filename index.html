About Us

Incorporated in 2018, Falcon Ventures Limited (FVL) operates with a strong passion and entrepreneurial spirit in mining, mineral exports, logistics, and infrastructure development. Managed by a highly qualified and experienced team, FVL specializes in mining high and medium grade manganese ores (42% to 50%+), ensuring quality through stringent sorting and segregating practices that meet global standards. Equipped with in-house facilities for sampling, sample preparation, wet analysis labs, and XRF technology, FVL caters to the needs of Ferro Manganese smelters, Silica Manganese smelters, Electrolytic Manganese Dioxide manufacturers, and chemical industries. Committed to sustainable development and environmental responsibility, FVL employs conscientious mining practices. Since its establishment, FVL has consistently delivered quality ores with reliable on-time delivery. Additionally, FVL plans to implement comprehensive Corporate Social Responsibility initiatives focused on medical welfare, education, infrastructure development, and cultural activities.
